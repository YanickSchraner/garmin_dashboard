# Product Guide: Garmin Custom Dashboard

## Overview
A personalized dashboard designed for runners and health enthusiasts to visualize Garmin Connect data, track annual running goals, and monitor weekly health performance through a custom web interface.

## Target Audience
- **Runners/Athletes**: Focused on achieving consistency and tracking training milestones.
- **Health Enthusiasts**: Interested in long-term trends for sleep, resting heart rate (RHR), and general well-being.
- **Data Analysts/Nerds**: Users who want precise visualizations and custom views beyond the standard Garmin app.

## Core Goals
- **Goal Visualization**: Real-time tracking of the "104 runs per year" goal (averaging 2 runs per week).
- **Weekly Performance Summary**: A high-level view of the current week’s trainings, sleep quality, and RHR.
- **Line Plot Comparison**: Graphical representation of "Target Runs" vs. "Actual Runs" over the course of the year.

## Key Features
- **Garmin API Integration**: Automated data fetching from Garmin Connect API.
- **Weekly View Panel**: A dedicated interface for reviewing the past week’s activity and health metrics.
- **Health Trend Tracking**: Visualization of improving health stats (e.g., lower RHR, better sleep duration) throughout the year.
- **Configurable Cron Updates**: A flexible, cron-like system for scheduling data fetches (e.g., daily at 4 AM or every 6 hours).
